Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

KoombaDrive requests access to your Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, and Amazon S3 accounts solely to sync files to your local filesystem. We do not collect, store, or share your files or personal data with any third party.

How we use your data

Your cloud storage credentials are stored locally on your device in the system Keychain. They are never transmitted to KoombaTechnology servers.

Third party services

KoombaDrive connects directly to Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, and Amazon S3 APIs on your behalf. Your use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@koombatechnology.com
